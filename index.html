Every AYCARS Event

One Format.
Three Parts.
Every Time.

01
Act One
The Meetup

Members gather at the sponsor's location — showroom, boutique, restaurant, or office. They spend 1–3 hours directly engaging with the sponsor and their team. This is not a logo placement. This is your target market standing in your space.

1–3 hours · Direct interaction · CEOs, Entrepreneurs & HNW individuals
02
Act Two
The Drive

40+ supercars take to the roads in convoy — Bugattis, McLarens, Lamborghinis, Ferraris, and Porsches moving together. Every drive produces naturally viral content captured by members and influencers, reaching millions across platforms.

273M Instagram Views · 127M TikTok Views · Organic viral reach
03
Act Three
The Final Activity

Events close with a premium shared experience — luxury dinners, networking events, sports activities, or business workshops. Memories formed here permanently associate the sponsor's brand with that feeling.

Luxury Dinners · Sports · Networking · Business Workshops
