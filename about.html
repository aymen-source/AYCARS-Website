AYCARS is where supercar owners, entrepreneurs, and high-net-worth individuals come together. We host events in Dubai and Miami where 40+ exotic cars gather, powerful engines meet powerful minds, and real business gets done.

We operate at the intersection of three things: an exclusive supercar club, a high-value networking organisation, and a media brand with hundreds of millions of views. Cars are our gateway. Relationships are our product.

The People Behind It

Our Founder

Aymen Yasin
Founder · Dubai & Miami

Aymen grew up as a global citizen — a young entrepreneur from Pakistan who has lived across eight countries and absorbed the cultures, business mindsets, and social networks of each. He arrived in the Dubai supercar scene with a different perspective: not just a love for the cars, but a sharp understanding of how social media, community, and shared passion could be turned into something genuinely transformative.

He saw that existing clubs were events businesses — not communities. AYCARS was built to be different: a place where being a supercar owner is the starting point, not the finish line.

Lived across 8 countries · Social media & community expert · Dubai & Miami supercar scene
Miami Expansion

AYCARS
Comes to
Miami.

AYCARS is bringing its curated supercar and networking experience to Miami, one of the world's most vibrant cities for automotive culture, entrepreneurship, and high-net-worth communities.

The Miami chapter operates in partnership with Top Tier Miami Club, the city's leading supercar community. Together, we're creating the same high-energy, high-connection event format that has made AYCARS a fixture in Dubai, now expanding to American soil.

Miami events follow the same format: a curated meetup at a premium venue, a convoy drive through the city, and a closing experience: dinner, networking, or an exclusive activity. Same standard. New city. New connections.

> Miami Chapter Partner
Top Tier Miami Club

Top Tier Miami Club is Miami's premier automotive lifestyle community — connecting supercar owners, entrepreneurs, and luxury enthusiasts through curated events across South Florida. Their network and local expertise make them the natural home for AYCARS in the United States.
